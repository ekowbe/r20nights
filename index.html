What Is R20

A space for the questions everyone's afraid to ask.

College is a season of enormous pressure and enormous possibility. R20 Nights exists for students navigating all of it — the academic weight, the social searching, the quiet questions about purpose and meaning that don't fit neatly into a syllabus.

R20 is a campus ministry that invests in young people across Manhattan. On Saturday nights at Columbia, we bring that community to your campus.

01
Live Worship

A genuine musical experience — not background noise. Songs that give language to what you're feeling, whether or not you've ever sung in a church before.

02
Honest Preaching

Real messages for real life. We tackle the questions people actually have — doubt, suffering, identity, the future — without pretending the answers are easy.

03
Community After

Coffee, desserts, and actual conversation. The hour after the service is where most people decide to come back — because they met someone worth knowing.

Plan My Visit

What to expect on your first visit

  • ♫
    Worship
    A live band leads 20–25 minutes of music. Sing along, stand, sit — whatever works for you. No pressure, no performance.
  • ✦
    The Message
    A 25-minute talk grounded in scripture — practical, honest, and built around questions real people actually carry.
  • ☕
    Community Time
    Coffee, desserts, and real conversation. A prayer station is available if you want one-on-one prayer before you leave.
  • ◎
    No Awkward Moments
    We won't ask first-time visitors to stand, speak, or do anything uncomfortable. You're here to explore — that's enough.
