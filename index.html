Who We Are

A space for the questions everyone's afraid to ask.

College is a season of enormous pressure and enormous possibility. We started R20 because some of us were navigating all of it — the academic weight, the social searching, the quiet questions about purpose and meaning that don't fit neatly into a syllabus — and figured we'd rather do it together than alone.

We're students across Columbia, NYU, CCNY, and Pace — and growing. Bible Hangouts on campus during the week, and Saturday nights at Columbia where we all come together.

01
Music

We sing together. A band leads — sit, stand, sing along, or just listen. Whether you've done this a thousand times or never, it's fine.

02
The Talk + Q&A

Honest messages for actual life — doubt, suffering, identity, the future — then we open the floor. Ask whatever's on your mind. No question off-limits.

03
Hanging Out

Coffee, desserts, and real conversation after. The hour after is where most of us actually meet each other. If something's heavy and you want prayer, we're around for that too.

Join Us Saturdays

What it actually looks like

  • ♫
    Music
    A live band leads 20–25 minutes. Sing along, stand, sit — whatever works for you. No pressure, no performance.
  • ✦
    The Talk
    About 25 minutes — practical, honest, and built around the questions students actually carry.
  • ?
    Open Q&A
    After the talk, we open the floor. Ask whatever's on your mind — about what was just said, or about faith in general. No question off-limits, nothing rehearsed.
  • ☕
    Hanging Out
    Coffee, desserts, and real conversation after. Stay as long or as little as you want.
  • +
    Prayer, if You Want It
    If something's heavy and you want someone to pray with you, we're around. No spotlight, no expectations — just ask, or don't.
  • ◎
    No Awkward Moments
    We won't ask first-timers to stand, speak, or do anything uncomfortable. You're here to check it out — that's enough.

Connect

First time is the hardest part.

Showing up somewhere new is a real thing to do. We know — most of us were in your spot at some point. We try to make the first step easy, and the second step worth taking.

No follow-up spam. We'll reach out sparingly — a friendly nudge, not a campaign. That's it, unless you want more.

No pressure to join anything. Come hang out. Enjoy the coffee. See how you feel.

Questions welcome. Doubt doesn't disqualify you here. It's actually why we exist.

First time here? It's on us. Grab some free R20 merch on your way out — just for showing up.
